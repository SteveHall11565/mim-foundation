Our Definition
Stewardship is the active, values-driven management of powerful resources in ways that account for their impact on stakeholders beyond the immediate transaction — not as a PR posture, but as a core operating principle.

All Make It Matter Foundation publications are grounded in this definition and the belief that stewardship capitalism is not CSR, ESG, or philanthropic window dressing. It is how capitalism was always intended to work.
